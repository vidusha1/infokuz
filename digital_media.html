DIGITAL BROCHURES

InFokuz provides visually captivating digital interactive flyers and brochures to promote your business. Our brochures and flyers possess interactive capabilities enabling you to link to your website, email, virtual tour, Google Maps and more.

Create powerful brochures that spark customer interest with high resolution photography showcasing your business and/or space.

Included are your company’s logo, disclaimer and any detailed information that you provide from one of our assorted layouts. Digital brochures and flyers guarantee visually captivating designs that grab the attention of your clients while also providing a professional look that conveys your powerful message.

Experts in the Real Estate industry, we distribute messages to other professionals in an appealing format with our own designs. DIGITAL PHOTOGRAPHY

InFokuz photographers specialize in architectural images to enhance your interior and exterior space bringing out the best features of your building and the surrounding area using high resolution interior and exterior photographs.

Our extensive real estate and business expertise enables us to produce quality imagery to reflect the unique aspects of your building using eye-catching photography.

Construction photography is a great marketing tool and we provide this to reference your site’s build and track its progress. A combination of ground view photography and aerial images gives greater detail of a project as it unfolds.

Customized images are provided in a timely manner, in most cases within 24 hours, enabling you to move quickly with your project. All imagery is expertly reviewed and retouched to give the best results.
