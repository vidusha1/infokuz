360° VIRTUAL TOURS

The Matterport Pro 3D camera works by generating 4K 360° stills while scanning the space around it. This rotation allows the camera to reflect every detail of the space and create accurate measurements that provide unimaginable detail.

Matterport technology also enables 2D schematic floor plans to be generated with accuracy and detail. Matterport’s digital twins capture space with crisp 4K high-resolution quality enabling guided tours to be created that highlight the best features of your space through photos and video. Labels and tags are another great feature that provide the ability to showcase information and enhance viewer experience.

We upload the content for further processing with a fast turnaround time, which is typically 24 hours. Our rapid service enables you to view the final product quickly and present it to your clients. The end result embeds a variety of media into your website, including digital interactive brochures and social media platforms. It can also be shared via a streaming link

InFokuz uses the latest Matterport 3D technology to deliver realistic results to promote your business while also targeting realtors needs. With digital 3D camera technology that captures 360° panoramic detail of your space from every angle, we provide the most advanced in-depth view for the ultimate virtual tour experience. The 3D perspective engages your clients with a walk-through experience of your location that allows you to draw in more business and make more bookings.
